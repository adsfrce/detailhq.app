FAQ

Short, honest, no marketing fluff.

Wie funktioniert die 7 days Free trial?
You sign up and get full access immediately. After 7 days you decide: keep going or stop. No credit card required.
Is this really built for wrap shops?
Ja. Die Abläufe sind auf Detailing und Folierung ausgelegt – also Services, Zeiten, Jobs, Status und saubere Customerndaten.
Gibt es Appointment reminders?
Yes. Use reminders to reduce no-shows and cut down follow-ups.
Is this a POS system?
Nein. Du kannst den Zahlungsstatus am Job erfassen (paid/open/Teilzahlung), aber es ist kein POS/Kassensystem.
Monthly oder jährlich – kann ich wechseln?
Yes. You can switch between monthly and yearly later.
What exactly is the demo?
An interactive, read-only version. Click through everything, but nothing is saved. Perfect to feel the UI.

Premium service requires premium workflows.
